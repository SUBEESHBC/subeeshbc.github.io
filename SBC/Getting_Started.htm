Reader

A Reader can visit the main page of the Online Journal; search and download articles by entering author name, category or keyword. Following are the prerequisites expected of the Reader for performing the same:

  • Internet literacy
  • Ability to use search engine
  • Access to the Online Journal Website

    Author

    Authors can submit articles to the Online Journal Website, for consideration. Following are the prerequisites expected of the Author for performing the same:

  • Internet literacy
  • Access to the Online Journal Website
  • Ability to use e-mail with attachments

    Reviewer

    Reviewer can recommend approval for publication or suggest changes to an article assigned for review by the Editor of the Online Journal. Following are the prerequisites expected of the Reviewer for performing the same:

  • Historical Society membership
  • Internet literacy
  • Access to the Online Journal Website
  • Ability to use email with attachments
