Overview

Web Publishing System is a software designed to facilitate online publication of articles pertaining to regional history. The system provides an interface for Readers, Authors and Reviewers to search, submit and review articles, respectively. For readers, Web Publishing System serves as a means to search and download articles based on author name, category or keyword. For Authors and Reviewers, the system acts as a platform that assists in simplifying the process of article submission and review by means of emails.
