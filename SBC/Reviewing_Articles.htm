Reviewing Articles

Editor of the Journal redirects the articles submitted by Authors to the Reviewer, for reviewing. Reviewer can either approve the article for publication or suggest changes to be incorporated for improvement. Once the article is reviewed, the Reviewer sends it back to the Editor, along with recommendations. Following are the steps to be followed to send a reviewed article to the Editor:

Step 1: Connect to the Online Journal Website

Step 2: Click on the Email Editor buttonbutton; Reviewer’s email client window pops up

test test test

Step 3: In the email window, fill in the subject line, attach the article file and click Send

You will receive an automated email acknowledgement from the Journal, once the process is successful.
